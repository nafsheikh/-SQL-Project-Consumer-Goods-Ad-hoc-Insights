# 📊 Atliq Hardware Sales Analysis

## 🔹 Project Overview

This project analyzes **Atliq Hardware’s sales performance** using **SQL** for data extraction and **Power BI** for dashboard visualization.

The main objective is to solve business problems, identify sales trends, and generate useful insights for better decision-making.

---

## 🔹 Business Questions Covered

* 📈 Unique product increase in 2021 vs 2020
* 🗂 Segment-wise unique product counts
* 🚀 Segment with highest product increase
* 💰 Highest and lowest manufacturing cost products
* 🎯 Top 5 customers by average discount percentage
* 📅 Monthly gross sales report for Atliq Exclusive
* 📦 Quarter with maximum total sold quantity in 2020
* 🏪 Channel-wise gross sales contribution in 2021
* 🏆 Top 3 products in each division by sold quantity

---

## 🔹 Tools Used

* 🛢 SQL (MySQL)
* 📊 Power BI
* 📑 Excel

---

## 🔹 Key Insights

* 🏪 Retailer channel contributed the highest gross sales
* 💻 Notebook segment had the highest unique product count
* 📦 Q1 recorded the maximum total sold quantity
* 📅 November 2020 was the highest gross sales month for Atliq Exclusive
* 🚀 N & S division showed the strongest product demand
